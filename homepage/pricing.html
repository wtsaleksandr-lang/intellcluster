FIG A — FAQ
When does Pro billing start?
IntellCluster is in public beta. Pro access is available to waitlist members during launch. Billing begins once Stripe integration is live — you'll be notified at least 7 days before the first charge.
Can I use both Phronesis and Synthesis on one plan?
Yes. Every plan includes both tools. Pro/Team unlocks the expert modes and unlimited usage for both.
What happens if I cancel?
Your decisions remain accessible for 90 days after cancellation, then are archived. Export them any time before that.
Do you offer enterprise pricing?
Yes. Custom plans available for 50+ seats with API access, SLAs, and data residency. Contact via the waitlist form and mention enterprise.
