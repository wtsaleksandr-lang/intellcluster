INTELLCLUSTER · PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Decision and research inputs: The questions, options, criteria, and prompts you submit through Phronesis and Synthesis. We store these to deliver results and enable shareable history.

Usage data: Run IDs, timestamps, model selection, latency, and cost metrics. No personally identifying information is required to use the tools anonymously.

Waitlist email: If you join a Pro or Team waitlist, your email is stored to notify you when billing opens.

How we use it

Your inputs are sent to the AI providers we use (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, xAI) solely to generate your results. Those providers have their own privacy policies and retention rules.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your decision content with third parties beyond the AI providers required to generate results.

Retention

Free tier: decisions and research runs are retained for 90 days. Pro/Team: retained indefinitely until you delete them or your account. Anonymous visitors' data is retained for 30 days.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time. Because the tools currently operate anonymously, you can also simply stop using the service.

Contact

For privacy questions, reach out via the waitlist form.
