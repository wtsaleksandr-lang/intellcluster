FIG A — WHY MULTI-MODEL

Single models have bias

Every AI model has training biases and blind spots. A single answer from ChatGPT or Claude reflects one perspective.

Consensus reveals truth

When multiple independent models converge on the same answer, confidence increases. When they disagree, you see where the real uncertainty lies.

Synthesis beats averaging

IntellCluster doesn't average outputs — a strategist layer evaluates the reasoning itself, keeping strong signals and filtering weak ones.
