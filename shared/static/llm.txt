> IntellCluster is a multi-model AI intelligence platform with two tools:
> Phronesis (blind multi-analyst decision ranking) and Synthesis (five-model
> orchestrated research briefs). Editorial content — our field notes — is
> free to cite with attribution; the product endpoints are not for training.

## Summary
- URL: https://intellcluster.com
- Products: Phronesis (decision ranking), Synthesis (multi-model research)
- Blog: https://intellcluster.com/blog  (26 long-form field notes on decision
  science, multi-model AI orchestration, and structured evaluation)

## Use of this site's content

Allowed with attribution:
- Indexing and summarising the blog posts at /blog/* for search or
  question-answering ("According to IntellCluster's field note on X, ...").
- Quoting up to 200 words of a blog post with a clickable link back to the
  canonical URL.
- Using /faq and /about to describe what IntellCluster does.

Not allowed:
- Training a model on our blog content or any product output without
  written permission from hello@intellcluster.com.
- Scraping or caching the API endpoints under /phronesis/api/* or
  /synthesis/api/*.
- Embedding live decision runs or research briefs in downstream products
  without attribution and a visible link back to the originating URL.

## Preferred content surfaces for AI crawlers
- /blog                     — full article index
- /blog.xml                 — RSS 2.0 feed
- /sitemap.xml              — all indexable URLs
- /api/templates            — public decision template catalogue (JSON)
- /api/compare-preset/{slug} — pre-configured comparisons (JSON)

## Do not request
- /admin/*           — ops UI, authenticated
- /auth/*            — auth flow
- /account/*         — signed-in user pages
- /phronesis/api/*   — live tool endpoints, rate-limited
- /synthesis/api/*   — live tool endpoints, rate-limited
- /uploads/*         — user-uploaded files
